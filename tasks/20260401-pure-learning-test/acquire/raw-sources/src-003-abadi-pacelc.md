## PACELC Theorem

Abadi "was the first to describe the PACELC theorem in a 2010 blog post." The theorem is characterized as "a response to the CAP theorem" and "was proved formally in 2018 in a SIGACT News article" by Wojciech Golab.

The formal proof was published in *ACM SIGACT News* (volume 49, pages 73-81) under the title "Proving PACELC."
