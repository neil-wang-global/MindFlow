# Knowledge Base Entry

## Type
- concept

## Summary
- In medias res is a narrative technique opening at a midpoint or climactic moment rather than the chronological beginning, first described by Horace (c. 19 BCE), creating immediate engagement through action, deferred exposition, flashbacks, and urgency.
