## [SRC-008] Knowledge management: A model for organizational learning

**Source Type**: Journal Article  
**Source URL**: https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S1467089502000398  
**Collection Time**: 2026-03-11  
**Publication Time**: 2002  

**Core Content**:
文章提出知识管理如何通过不同过滤机制把环境中的知识导入组织核心稳定流程，以产生组织价值。

**Key Quote from Original**:
摘要指出企业进行知识管理，是为了 “filtering knowledge into its core, stable processes where that knowledge can be used to produce value for the firm”。
